Course Overview

Welcome to the comprehensive course on Best Practice Feedback and Complaints Handling. This course will equip you with essential skills and knowledge to handle feedback and complaints effectively in professional environments.

Learning Objectives

Objective 1: Understand the importance of effective feedback handling and its impact on customer satisfaction.
Objective 2: Learn proven techniques for managing complaints professionally and constructively.
Objective 3: Develop skills for resolving conflicts and turning negative experiences into positive outcomes.
Objective 4: Implement best practices for documentation, follow-up, and continuous improvement.

Course Content

This interactive course covers:

  • Understanding Feedback: Types of feedback, importance, and impact on organizations
  • Complaint Management: Systematic approach to handling complaints effectively
  • Communication Skills: Active listening, empathy, and professional communication
  • Resolution Strategies: Problem-solving techniques and conflict resolution
  • Documentation: Proper recording and tracking of feedback and complaints
  • Follow-up Procedures: Ensuring resolution and preventing recurrence
